Welcome to the Infant Caregiver Project!

A developmental psychology laboratory led by Dr. Mary Dozier, Amy E. du Pont Chair of Child Development and Professor of Psychology at the University of Delaware.

  • Attachment and Biobehavioral Catch-up (ABC)
    Helps caregivers provide nurturing care and engage in synchronous interactions with their children.
  • Backed by Research
    We conduct basic and translational research with children and families.
  • Expanding Now
    Efforts have begun to disseminate the program at multiple sites across the nation and internationally.
