Infant Caregiver Project

The Infant Caregiver Project is a developmental psychology laboratory led by Dr. Mary Dozier, Amy E. du Pont Chair of Child Development and Professor of Psychology at the University of Delaware. We conduct basic and translational research with children and families. Specifically, our research focuses on Attachment and Biobehavioral Catch-up (ABC), which helps caregivers provide nurturing care and engage in synchronous interactions with their children. In light of the evidence supporting the effectiveness of ABC, efforts have begun to disseminate the program at multiple sites across the nation and internationally.
